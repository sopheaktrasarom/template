About The Cedar

The Cedar is a striking new residential development in Damansara Heights, designed by award winning architect, Hamiltons International. With just sixty apartments and ten penthouses, this low density, low rise development provides the ultimate in comfort, privacy and peace of mind.

The development of The Cedar is managed by Tribeca Real Estate Asset Management Sdn Bhd. Tribeca is an innovative real estate advisory firm with strategies founded on years of experience in the field. Tribeca strives to push boundaries and set new standards of excellence collaborating with industry leaders in architecture, design, landscaping and civil engineering.
